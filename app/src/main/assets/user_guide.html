Subjects

The app allows for the recording of how much time is spent studying per subject. To do that it keeps information of each subject. This allows the user to see how much progress has been made as well to receive notifications if not enough progress has been made

It is important to note that this is a helping app.
It is able to store subject information of each subject.
This is done as it allows for better usability,
The system can store information for each subject
in the form of subject title, weight (importance),
effort (hours needed to be spent studying),
starting date as well as the ending date.

Adding a subject

The process of adding a new subject in the app is quite simple. It requires the user to click on the Add a Subject option in the bottom navbar. Then the user will be taken in the Add a Subject Page. There they will input all the relevant information. And click the save button.

Add a Subject Page.


Warning

If you have opted to use the Backup feature in that device the subject will not be saved until you have recorded your first session.

Modifying an existing subject

In some cases the wrong data will be inputted in the database when entering a new subject or some of the subject specification changes.

In order to accommodate those changes you will need to go to the Subjects page, click on the subject that you want to modify. Then on the subject page click the pencil button. There you will go to a page where you can modify the subject information.
Once you have completed the modification of the information you can click on the save button

Modify Subject Page.

Deleting a Subject

Sometimes a subject in the subject list may need to be removed. There are multiple ways to delete it.

1. Through the swipe to delete in the Subject Page
2. Through the Subject Overview Page

Subject Page

Swiping to the the right in the Subject page will delete the subject. Just click delete in the dialog box that will come up

Confirm deletion through
the Subjects Page

Subject Overview Page

By clicking the bin button you will be prompted to click whether or not you would want to delete the subject you are seeing. Just click delete in the dialog box that will come up and the subject will be deleted.

Confirm deletion through
the Subject Overview Page

Recording a session

Recording studying sessions is the most important part of the system.
There are two ways of starting a recording;
1. Through the recording button
2. Through the Subject Page

Recording Page

Recording Button

Press the recording button and you will be guided to a page with all your Subjects.
There you can choose the subject that you want to record. By just clicking it, the recording will start. Just press the center button when you are done and the recording will stop and be stored.

Recording Page where you can select
the subject you want to record

Subject Page

You can start a recording through the Subject Overview Page. Just click the plus button. Once done just click the save button.

Subject Overview Page where you can press
the plus button to start a recording session
on that subject

Deleting a recording

Sometimes accidental recordings occur and as such they need to be removed.
To do that just swipe right to one of the recordings in the Subject Overview Page.
Then you will be prompted to confirm the deletion. Press delete to confirm it.

Deleting a recording through
the Subject Overview Page

Use Backup

Warning

In order to use this feature you need to already have a pre-existing account in the application

The use of Firebase in the app is in order to provide a backup system to the user in case something happens to their original device.
It allows for an easy and seamless backup functionality.
Automatically backing up the subjects when a change occurs as well as deleting the subjects that have been deleted.

Warning

This feature should be used as a last resort method.
Please log in to your account before you use this feature.
Failure to do so will resort in inaccessibility to the feature and the need to reset the application
Thus losing all your data


Login to the Backup

In order to login to the Backup feature you will need to click to the three dots up in right corner and select Login to Backup.
There enter your credentials and press login.
If your credentials are wrong a pop up box will be shown.
Please ensure you have an internet connection
If they are correct your previous saved lessons will be loaded.
If you have used the application before your application data will need to be reset.

Login Page for the Backup Functionality

Backing up

The backing up procedure is made Automatically after a change has been made in the application data.

Data Reset

Sometimes you may want to perform a data reset.
This operation can easily be performed in the Login Page for the use of the Backup feature.
Just click on the Reset Button and click confirm in the dialog box.
The data reset action will then run.
